FAQs

Quick answers to common questions

How do I earn belts?
Complete all table levels within the time limit. Each belt has multiple tables to master. Pass all tables to unlock the next belt. You have 3 attempts per belt.
How do I find a tutor?
After logging in, go to "Learning Hub" → "Find a Tutor". Search by county or specialisation, send a request. Once accepted, you can chat and schedule video calls.
How do video calls work?
Click the video call button in chat to create a Google Meet link. Share the link with your tutor/student and join the call. No additional software needed.
What are assignments?
Tutors create custom assignments with questions. Students submit answers, tutors grade them. Perfect for homework and extra practice.
